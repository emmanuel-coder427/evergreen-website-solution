We create value at the heart of the circular economy

By giving new life to used materials, we help protecting the world for generations to come.

Being a leading waste-to-product company in Kenya most advanced circular economies, we contribute to a sustainable society for all of our stakeholders: customers, suppliers, local communities, employees, regulators, governments, investors and lenders.

Advancing circularity together. Now and in the future.

As a pure-play recycling company, we generate revenue by providing waste management services as well as converting most of the waste we collect into circular materials, which we sell to our customers. We connect actors in the circular economy to develop new solutions together, enabling our partners to realise their circular ambitions.
